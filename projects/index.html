In the Green

At the University of Cambridge's HackCambridge, my team and I created a front-end webapp with the goal of updating users on key sustainability issues in their stock portfolio to help them make more informed decisions about their investments. (January 2020) Our webapp displays a personal ‘investment tree’ linked with a stock portfolio, and tracks fund composition and webscrapes environmental, social and corporate governance (ESG) risk scores. We also included a chatbot feature to answer questions the user may have about sustainable investment. This project won 2nd place for the Microsoft Challenge at HackCambridge.
