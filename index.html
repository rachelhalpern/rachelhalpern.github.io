Projects

InTheGreen

HackCambridge

My team and I built a webapp frontend that displays a user's personal "investment tree" linked with the user's stock portfolio. A Python backend hosted on Microsoft Azure tracks the fund composition in real-time and webscrapes environmental, social and corporate governance (ESG) risk scores from Yahoo Finance. A weighted average and other comparisons are computed and made available to the webapp through a Flask-API.

Built with: azure, beautiful-soup, css, flask, html, javascript, json, numpy, pandas, python, regex

Won 2nd place in the Microsoft Hack For Good Challenge

Office Hours Redesigned

Design@Columbia

My team and I sought to redesign the model of office hours for computer science courses at Columbia University. We looked to improve time efficiency and honor COVID social-distancing guidelines while retaining the working features of office hours. We sketched and designed several solutions; our mockup highlights the main feature, a queue, for students to receive push notifications when it's their turn to ask questions. Next steps include researching with professors, implementing an announcement feature, and developing a desktop version.
